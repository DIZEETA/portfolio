Executive Summary

This analysis turns raw sales data into operational recommendations. Over the reporting period the pizzeria generated $65,160 from 1,685 orders, with customers ordering an average of 2.35 pizzas per order. Key peaks occur on Fridays and Saturdays and during lunch (12 PM) and dinner (5–6 PM). Classic pizzas and large sizes drive the most revenue.
