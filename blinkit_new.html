Project Overview

This project explores the drivers of sales performance across outlet types, locations, sizes, and product attributes (e.g., fat content, item categories). The workflow integrates data cleaning and transformation, exploratory analysis, and dashboard storytelling.

Power BI Python (Pandas, Matplotlib) Retail Analytics EDA + KPI Design

Key Insights

Low Fat items outsell Regular across tiers.
Medium outlet size hits the retail sweet spot.

Narrative Summary

Overall sales cross the $1.2M mark with healthy average order values and solid ratings. Sales momentum peaks in 2019 and remains steady afterwards. Large-format supermarkets dominate category performance, while Tier 3 geographies emerge as growth engines. A clear health-leaning pattern is visible as Low Fat products lead in every outlet tier. Medium-sized stores balance assortment and accessibility, delivering the best sales outcomes.
